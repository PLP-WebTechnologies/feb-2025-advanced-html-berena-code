Purpose Of The Study Group

This study group is meant to assist all members throughout their journey in learning HTML for the next 2 months of the program. The following are the benefits of joining the group:

  1. Improved understanding. You will learn different approaches to coding and problem solving.
  2. Collaborative learning. You will get to exchange ideas and clarify any doubts with your peers.
  3. Hands on practice. Working as a group in projects and real wolrd examples.
  4. Confidence building. You will gain confidence through peer to peer feedback and discussions amongst each other.
  5. Networking. Connecting with like minded learners for future support and other opportunities.
  6. Accountability. Staying motivated and consistent with coursework.
  7. Fun. Enjoying the learning process and making new friends.
